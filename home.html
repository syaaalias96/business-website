A REFUGE FOR SOCIAL GATHERING

Drama Cafe opened in 2020 as a cosy cafe hidden within Projectshop clothing store. This charming and understated spot quickly gained a following for its hearty takes on savoury cafe classics, famous truffle shoestring fries and indulgent cakes, puddings and tarts. Thoughtfully detailed interiors, lush surroundings, friendly relaxed service and generous portions have made this homegrown Kuala Lumpur brand an internationally recognized local favourite. Drama Cafe is the place for legendary brunches and lazy afternoon teas, romantic dates and friendly celebrations in beautiful surroundings


EVERYDAY NEIGHBOURHOOD GOURMET

Drama Cafe is a gourmet takeaway and retail concept that offers traditionally fast foods like pizzas, burgers and hearty salads prepared with a Slow Food sensibility. Guests can pre-order online for takeaway or over the counter at the store.

By working closely with over 20 different suppliers, the coffee shop offers a curated selection of coffees usually only offered to and available in restaurants, giving customers access to a new selection of great different coffee taste previously unavailable for retail purchase. A number of coffees are available for tasting.
