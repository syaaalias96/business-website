BACKGROUND OF THE COMPANY

The company was established in mid-2020 at 215, Jalan Imbi, Imbi, 55100 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur. The company is a business registered under the business act 1956. The company has invested a capital of RM 50,000 and the capital was collected from several of their partners. As a result of the capital accumulation and also a small loan from MARA, a company called "Drama Cafe" was built as a result of the agreement of all members. Each company has their own vision and mission. Our company's mission is "To achieve customer needs and the best service to achieve customer comfort" and our vision is "To achieve this mission, we will provide a comfortable place or space and privacy for our valued customers". Prior to the founding of the company, several studies were conducted to achieve good durability. Among the studies conducted are how to mix properly, test the quality of raw material to suit our taste and also the demonstration of dishes for our customers.

SUPPLIER

The backbone of every success of a company is having a good relationship with their raw material suppliers, as well as this company. Their greatest supplier is from Guatemala. Guatemala is a country famous for its high quality coffee production. Coffee is considered one of the best types of coffee in the world because it grows in mountainous areas which gives a more intense taste, tart, and influenced by the climatic conditions in which it is grown. The famous type of coffee is "Antigua Volcanic" which has a sophisticated taste, quite heavy as well as a strong and delicate aroma with smoky. Due to the frequent influence of sea breezes, this Guatemalan coffee sometimes has a mild taste with a strong acidic tone. The second supplier is from Costa Rica. Costa Rican coffee has a classic taste and has a growing reputation in volcanic soils making it smoother, softer and has a rich walnut flavor, Coffee in general is a processed arabica.
